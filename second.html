Spicy Black Bean Tacos

25 mins | 4 Servings | Medium Spice

Quick and delicious tacos filled with spicy black beans, fresh vegetables, and zesty toppings. A vibrant vegetarian meal perfect for weeknights.

Vegetarian Mexican

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt to taste
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Fresh lime wedges for serving
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, sauté until soft.
  2. Add black beans, cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and salt. Cook for 5–7 minutes, lightly mashing some beans for texture.
  3. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  4. Fill each tortilla with the bean mixture, lettuce, tomato, and red onion.
  5. Top with sour cream or yogurt, sprinkle cilantro, and serve with fresh lime wedges.
